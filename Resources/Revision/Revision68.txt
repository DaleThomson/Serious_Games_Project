Rule 156:

Do not park in passing places.

Rule 157:

Certain motorised vehicles do not meet the construction and technical requirements for road vehicles and are
generally not intended, not suitable and not legal for road, pavement, footpath, cycle path or bridleway use.
These include most types of miniature motorcycles, also called mini motos, and motorised scooters, also called go
peds, which are powered by electric or internal combustion engines. These types of vehicle MUST NOT be used on roads,
pavements, footpaths or bridleways.
Laws RTA 1988 sects 34, 41a, 42, 47, 63 & 66, HA 1835, sect 72, & R(S)A sect 129
