Rule 102:

Children in cars, vans and other goods vehicles. Drivers who are carrying children in cars, vans and other goods
vehicles should also ensure that:

   - children should get into the vehicle through the door nearest the kerb
   - child restraints are properly fitted to manufacturer's instructions
   - children do not sit behind the rear seats in an estate car or hatchback, unless a special child seat has been
     fitted
   - the child safety door locks, where fitted, are used when children are in the vehicle
     children are kept under control.
