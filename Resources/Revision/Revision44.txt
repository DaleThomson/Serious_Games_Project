Rule 105:

You MUST obey signals given by police officers, traffic officers, traffic wardens and signs used by school
crossing patrols.
Laws RTRA sect 28, RTA 1988 sect 35, TMA 2004 sect 6, & FTWO art 3

Rule 106:

Police stopping procedures. If the police want to stop your vehicle they will, where possible, attract your
attention by:

   - flashing blue lights, headlights or sounding their siren or horn, usually from behind
   - directing you to pull over to the side by pointing and/or using the left indicator.

You MUST then pull over and stop as soon as it is safe to do so. Then switch off your engine.
Law RTA 1988 sect 163
