Rule 111:

Never assume that flashing headlights is a signal inviting you to proceed. Use your own judgement and proceed
carefully.

Rule 112:

The horn. Use only while your vehicle is moving and you need to warn other road users of your presence. Never sound
your horn aggressively. You MUST NOT use your horn

   -while stationary on the road
   -when driving in a built-up area between the hours of 11.30 pm and 7.00 am

except when another road user poses a danger.
