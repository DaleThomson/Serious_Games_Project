Rule 109:

Traffic light signals and traffic signs. You MUST obey all traffic light signals and traffic signs giving orders, 
including temporary signals & signs. Make sure you know, understand and act on all other traffic and information 
signs and road markings.
Laws RTA 1988 sect 36 & TSRGD regs 10, 15, 16, 25, 26, 27, 28, 29, 36, 38 & 40

Rule 110:

Flashing headlights. Only flash your headlights to let other road users know that you are there. Do not flash your
headlights to convey any other message or intimidate other road users.
