   -if you do not give your name and address at the time of the collision, report it to the police as soon as

Rule 287:

If another person is injured and you do not produce your insurance certificate at the time of the crash to a police
officer or to anyone having reasonable grounds to request it, you MUST

   -report it to the police as soon as possible and in any case within 24 hours
   -produce your insurance certificate for the police within seven days.
    Law RTA 1988 sect 170
